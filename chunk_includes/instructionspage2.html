You will be presented a series of straightforward statements that we would like you to evaluate as True or False . The statements concern categorizations, such as All socks are clothes . Each statement will scroll out — one word at a time, like words on a page — and the relevant keys for answering are D (for True) and K (for False). Note that it is important to us that you answer in a timely fashion. That is, while you are not pressured to answer fast we are seeking your immediate reactions.
